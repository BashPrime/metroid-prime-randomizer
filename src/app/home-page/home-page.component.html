Welcome!

Metroid Prime Randomizer is an item randomizer for Metroid Prime (for the Nintendo GameCube). Once all of the core features are implemented, you will be able to randomize and patch a Metroid Prime .iso from the comfort of your web browser.

Click on Generate Randomized Game above to get started.

What is a randomizer? How does it work?

The idea is that all 100 item locations in the game - upgrades, Chozo Artifacts, and expansions - are shuffled around. You never know what you're going to find, and that's what makes a randomizer so much fun to play! Depending on what items you get, your exploration through Tallon IV can be (drastically) different from game to game.
