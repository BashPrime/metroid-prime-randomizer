Game Logics

There are logic rules in place which prevent you from being locked out of progression and guaranteeing that youâ€™ll be able to finish the game every time regardless of the distribution of items. The different game modes use different logic and assume different levels of game knowledge and ability.

No Glitches

This mode requires no advanced knowledge about the game, and is ideal for casual players.

This logic is designed to prevent players from getting stuck in as many locations as possible, but it is still possible to softlock when you enter a room that you're unable to leave (ex: vanilla beam rooms without the necessary beam to escape, Elite Quarters without obtaining X-Ray visor).

Easy Glitches (to be added)

This mode accounts for some of the easier-to-execute glitches, techniques, and sequence breaks in the game. They are as follows:

  • Dashing (such as scan dashing)
  • Slope Jumping
  • L Jumping
  • Visor skips for puzzles (such as power conduits, invisible platforms)
  • Exploiting collision you can stand on (allows access to rooms and items without Spider Ball, Grapple Beam, etc.)

You will never be forced to do any of the following:

  • NSJ (no space jump) techniques in rooms that expect you to have Space Jump Boots equipped (Pirate labs, Phazon Mines, etc.)
  • Underwater slope jumps without Space Jump Boots
  • Infinite Speed glitch
  • Traversing Magmoor Caverns without a suit
  • Traversing Twin Fires Tunnel by scan dashing or R jumping
  • Out of bounds sequences
  • Tricks requiring you to go out of bounds (such as floaty jump)
  • Bars skip in Great Tree Hall
  • Life Grove/Life Grove Tunnel without Boost Ball
  • Geothermal Core puzzle without Boost Ball
  • Exit Gravity Chamber without Gravity Suit
  • Exit Quarantine Cave without Spider Ball or Grapple Beam
  • Quarantine Monitor without Spider Ball or Grapple Beam
  • Crashed Frigate without Gravity Suit or Wave Beam
  • Phazon Mines without Spider Ball
  • Spider Ball skips in Phazon Mines, such as spiderless shafts
  • Magma Pool, Training Chamber Access, and Training Chamber without Grapple Beam
  • "Early Wild" Infinite Bomb Jump
