Game Logics

There are logic rules in place which prevent you from being locked out of progression and guaranteeing that youâ€™ll be able to finish the game every time regardless of the distribution of items. The different game modes use different logic and assume different levels of game knowledge and ability.

No Glitches

This mode requires no advanced knowledge about the game, and is ideal for casual players.

This logic is designed to prevent players from getting stuck in as many locations as possible, but it is still possible to softlock when you enter a room that you're unable to leave (ex: vanilla beam rooms without the necessary beam to escape, Elite Quarters without obtaining X-Ray visor).

Easy Glitches (to be added)

This mode accounts for some of the easier-to-execute glitches, techniques, and sequence breaks in the game. They are as follows:

  • Dashing (such as scan dashing)
  • Slope Jumping
  • L Jumping
  • Visor skips for puzzles (such as power conduits, invisible platforms)
  • Exploiting collision you can stand on (allows access to rooms and items without Spider Ball, Grapple Beam, etc.)

You will never be forced to do any of the following:

  • NSJ (no space jump) techniques in rooms that expect you to have Space Jump Boots equipped (Pirate labs, Phazon Mines, etc.)
  • Underwater slope jumps without Space Jump Boots
  • Infinite Speed glitch
  • Traversing Magmoor Caverns without a suit
  • Traversing Twin Fires Tunnel by scan dashing or R jumping
  • Out of bounds sequences, or tricks requiring you to go out of bounds
  • Bars skip in Great Tree Hall
  • Life Grove/Life Grove Tunnel without Boost Ball
  • Geothermal Core puzzle without Boost Ball
  • Exit Gravity Chamber without Gravity Suit
  • Exit Quarantine Cave without Spider Ball or Grapple Beam
  • Quarantine Monitor without Spider Ball or Grapple Beam
  • Crashed Frigate without Gravity Suit or Wave Beam
  • Phazon Mines without Spider Ball
  • Spider Ball skips in Phazon Mines, such as spiderless shafts
  • Root Cave, Magma Pool, Training Chamber Access, and Training Chamber without Grapple Beam
  • "Early Wild" Infinite Bomb Jump

Moderate Glitches

This mode accounts for more significant sequence breaks and more difficult tricks than Easy Glitches:

  • All dashes with Space Jump equipped
  • R Jumping
  • Using some of the easier NSJ (No Space Jump) movement in a few rooms
  • Underwater slope jumps without Space Jump equipped
  • "Early Wild" Infinite Bomb Jump (IBJ) in Sun Tower
  • The following uses of the Infinite Speed glitch:
    • "Early Sun" IS (in Chapel of the Elders)
    • Magma Pool IS
  • Bars skip in Great Tree Hall
  • Climbing Frigate Crash Site (with Space Jump)
  • Grapple Beam is no longer required if you have Space Jump equipped
  • Skipping Boost Ball, Spider Ball, and Gravity Suit almost everywhere (see below for constraints)
  • Boost Ball is only required for Life Grove, Life Grove Tunnel, and Plasma Processing
  • Traversing most of Magmoor Caverns without a suit upgrade. The logic required a minimum of 5 Energy Tanks
  • If "Allow Out-of-Bounds" is checked, the following wallcrawls:
      Wave/Sun IS
      Arbor Chamber
      Training Chamber/Training Chamber Access
      Chozo Ruins floaty jump for Dynamo and Training Chamber IBJ
      Magmoor Caverns floaty jump for Geothermal Core IBJ

You will never be forced to do any of the following:

  • Most NSJ tricks, including:
    • Hyper Bomb Jump (HBJ)
    • Uber Bomb Jump (UBJ)
    • Half-pipe Bomb Jump (HPBJ)
    • Any tricks that skip using other upgrades such as Boost Ball, Spider Ball, Grapple Beam, etc.
  • Life Grove and Life Grove Tunnel without Boost Ball and Power Bombs
  • Plasma Processing without Boost Ball
  • Crashed Frigate without Gravity Suit
  • Far Phendrana (except Transport Access), Magmoor past the Tallon elevator, and Phazon Mines without Space Jump
  • Dynamo (inbounds), Phendrana Shorelines, and Ice Ruins East spider tracks without Spider Ball
  • Any of the above out-of-bounds wallcrawls without Space Jump, Morph Ball, and bombs equipped
